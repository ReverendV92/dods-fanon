Bomb stacks.

Modeled after Luftwaffe SC 250 bombs.

This .zip contains 3 models (prop_static): 

* bomb_stack_1
* bomb_stack_2
* bomb_stack_3

Each stack is about 1600 tris at LOD0 and 770 tris at LOD1 (30 meters) and uses a 512*512 texture (no normal map).
